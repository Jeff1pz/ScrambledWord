About Word Scramble

Welcome to Word Scramble, a fun and interactive game designed to challenge your vocabulary and quick thinking!

How It Works

The game presents you with scrambled words, and your goal is to unscramble them before time runs out. Choose a category and difficulty level, then race against time!

Game Features

📚 Multiple categories: English, Math, Science, History, and Arts.

⏳ Adjustable difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Hard.

🏆 A dynamic leaderboard to track the best players.

💡 Use hints to help unscramble tricky words.

🎵 Immersive sound effects and animations for an engaging experience.

Meet the Developers

We are a team of passionate developers and educators who believe in making learning fun. Our goal is to enhance cognitive skills while keeping players entertained!
